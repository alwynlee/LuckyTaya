🇵🇭 Philippines Regulatory Hiring Obligations
PAGCOR Licence
CCO and Compliance Officer must be named on the licence. PAGCOR can audit without notice. Both must be in place before soft launch.
AMLC (R.A. 9160)
A designated AML Compliance Officer and operational STR filing process are mandatory. The AML/STR Officer is a non-negotiable Phase 1 hire.
NPC / R.A. 10173 (Data Privacy)
A registered DPO must be filed with the National Privacy Commission before player data is collected. Required at or before go-live.
BSP e-Money Regulations
GCash and Maya integrations require BSP-compliant payment processes. The Payments Specialist must understand BSP e-money rules for fund flow.
DOLE Labour Compliance
Once hiring locally in PH, DOLE registration plus SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG obligations apply. HR Manager needed before headcount reaches 20.
BIR Tax Compliance
All Philippine revenue is subject to BIR filings. A Financial Controller with BIR experience is required by Phase 2 at the latest.
