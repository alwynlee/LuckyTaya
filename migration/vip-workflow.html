Player Lifecycle — 7 Stages
01
Identification & Nomination
System flags or manual nomination · Before any VIP contact is made
CRM / Analyst
▾
Identification Triggers
  • Automated system flag: player deposits ≥ ₱50,000 in a single session or reaches monthly volume threshold for Bronze tier or above
  • Deposit velocity: 5 or more deposits within 7 days with aggregate value qualifying for Silver or above
  • Manual nomination by CS Team Lead or CRM Manager based on observed player behaviour — high engagement, large bet sizes, frequent play
  • Reactivation signal: previously inactive VIP returns and deposits at qualifying volume within their first 14 days back
  • Referral: an existing VIP refers a friend who deposits at qualifying levels — escalate referral for review
  • Cross-product play: player active across sportsbook, casino, and specialty games — indicates higher LTV potential even if single-product deposit is below threshold
🇵🇭 Philippines: A significant proportion of PH high-value players prefer GCash and Maya for deposits. Ensure the identification system tracks e-wallet deposit aggregates — not only bank transfers — as these are often split across multiple smaller transactions to avoid attention.
Nomination Process
  • CRM Analyst reviews the flagged player profile — verify KYC status is fully approved and EDD is complete if applicable before any VIP contact
  • Pull a 30-day play summary: total deposits, net gaming revenue (NGR), game mix, session frequency, and average session value
  • Assign a provisional VIP tier based on the deposit volume table and risk score from the Compliance team
  • Create a VIP nomination record in the CRM system with the supporting data and provisional tier — submit for VIP Manager approval
  • VIP Manager reviews the nomination within 4 hours and approves, declines, or adjusts the provisional tier
  • If approved: assign the player to an available VIP Account Manager and trigger the onboarding workflow (Stage 2)

VIP Onboarding
First contact within 24h of nomination approval · Preference capture · Relationship foundation
VIP Account Manager
▾
Onboarding Actions
  • Assigned VIP Account Manager (VAM) makes first contact within 24 hours of nomination approval — preferred channel is SMS or direct call depending on player preference
  • Introduce yourself by name, state your role as their dedicated host, and confirm the player's VIP tier and what this means for them specifically
  • Conduct a preference capture conversation: preferred contact time, preferred channel (SMS, Viber, WhatsApp, email), game preferences, favourite sports, birthdays and anniversaries
  • Confirm the player's preferred withdrawal method and note any preference for GCash, Maya, or bank transfer — flag to Payments team if a priority withdrawal lane is needed
  • Explain the cashback structure, withdrawal priority, and any immediate welcome gift or bonus available at their tier
  • Record all captured preferences in the VIP CRM profile within 2 hours of the conversation
  • Trigger the welcome gift or bonus within 24 hours of onboarding call completion
  • Schedule the first 7-day check-in in the host calendar
🇵🇭 Philippines: Viber is the dominant messaging platform for personal communication in the Philippines — not WhatsApp. Most PH VIP players will prefer Viber or SMS over email. Use the player's first name and a warm, respectful tone. Filipino culture values pakikisama (camaraderie) — building genuine rapport is more effective than a purely transactional approach.
Preference Capture Checklist
  • Preferred contact channel: Viber / SMS / WhatsApp / Email / Phone
  • Best contact time: morning / afternoon / evening
  • Game preferences: sportsbook, casino, specialty games, specific titles
  • Favourite sports and leagues (PBA, NBA, Premier League, boxing)
  • Birthday, wedding anniversary, and other key dates
  • Preferred withdrawal method and target turnaround expectation
  • Languages spoken: Filipino / English / regional dialect
  • Lifestyle interests for gifting: food, electronics, experiences, travel
  • Any responsible gambling preferences or limits to respect

Active Relationship Management
Ongoing touchpoint cadence · Personalised offers · Proactive engagement
VIP Account Manager
▾
Touchpoint Cadence by Tier
  • Bronze: Monthly check-in message. Automated CRM campaign. No personal host outreach required unless player initiates
  • Silver: Bi-weekly personal message from shared host. Monthly personalised bonus offer. Key date acknowledgement (birthday)
  • Gold: Weekly personal check-in via Viber/SMS. Bi-weekly personalised offer. Monthly gift (within budget). Quarterly invitation to VIP event or experience
  • Platinum: 2–3 personal contacts per week. Monthly gift and cashback review. Bi-annual in-person event or experience. Concierge assistance available on request
  • Elite: Daily availability from Senior VIP Manager. Bespoke monthly terms review. Priority on all services. Annual overseas experience or trip within approved budget
Proactive Engagement Triggers
  • Large win: contact within 2 hours to congratulate. Confirm withdrawal timeline and ensure experience is smooth
  • Large loss session: contact within 4 hours with a genuine check-in. Do not immediately offer a bonus — prioritise player welfare first. Offer cashback only if appropriate and within RG guidelines
  • Key dates: birthday, anniversary — send a personalised message and a tier-appropriate gift within the approved budget 1–2 days before
  • Withdrawal delay: if a VIP withdrawal is delayed beyond their tier SLA, proactively contact before the player complains — explain the delay and provide a revised ETA
  • New product launch: personally inform Gold+ players of new games or sportsbook markets before the general announcement — make them feel like insiders
  • Inactivity signal: if a Gold+ player has not logged in for 7 days, trigger a personal re-engagement message — do not wait for 14-day automated CRM trigger
🇵🇭 Philippines: Major local events and occasions to track for personalised outreach: PBA seasons, Pacquiao fight nights, UAAP basketball, Sinulog and Pasko season (Christmas is culturally significant for gifting), payday periods (15th and 30th of each month — highest deposit volumes), and local municipal fiestas for regional players.

04
Tier Review & Adjustment
Monthly review cycle · Upgrade, maintain, or downgrade · Player communication required
VIP Manager + CRM Analyst
▾
Monthly Review Process
  • CRM Analyst runs the monthly VIP tier review report on the last business day of each month — pulls 30-day deposit volumes for all active VIP players
  • Compare each player's actual 30-day NGR against their current tier threshold. Flag players who are eligible for upgrade or are at risk of downgrade
  • VIP Manager reviews the report and approves upgrade, maintain, or downgrade decisions within 2 business days
  • For downgrades: VAM must notify the player at least 7 days before the downgrade takes effect — explain the threshold, acknowledge their loyalty, and present a path back to their current tier
  • For upgrades: VAM notifies the player immediately and triggers the tier upgrade welcome package within 24 hours
  • Update the VIP CRM profile with the new tier, effective date, and the reviewing manager's name

05
Churn Risk & Reactivation
Early warning signals · Retention intervention · Reactivation campaign for lapsed VIPs
VAM + VIP Manager
▾
Churn Risk Signals
  • 7-day inactivity (Gold+): VAM sends personal check-in within 24h — ask if everything is okay, not just a bonus push
  • 14-day inactivity (Silver and below): automated CRM trigger. VAM personal outreach for Gold+
  • Deposit volume drop >50% month-over-month: VAM proactively reaches out to understand reason — player lifecycle event, competition, or product dissatisfaction
  • Unresolved complaint: any open CS or VIP complaint older than 48 hours is an immediate churn risk. Escalate to VIP Manager same day
  • Withdrawal delay complaint: treat as critical. VIP withdrawals delayed beyond SLA are the single biggest churn driver for high-value players
  • Social signal: player mentions competitor platform or product in communication — flag immediately to VIP Manager
Retention & Reactivation Actions
  • Immediate personal outreach from VAM — warm, genuine, not scripted. Ask open questions rather than pushing offers
  • Identify the root cause: product issue, personal circumstance, competitive offer, or unresolved problem. Document in CRM
  • If competitive offer cited: VAM escalates to VIP Manager with a counter-offer recommendation within 4 hours — approval required before any offer is made
  • If product complaint: VAM logs a formal product feedback ticket and informs the player that their feedback is being escalated to the product team
  • For lapsed VIPs (30+ days inactive): VIP Manager designs a personalised reactivation package — targeted free bet, cashback offer, or experience — to be presented by VAM in a personal call
  • All retention offers must be logged in the bonus engine with the player ID, offer value, VAM authorisation, and VIP Manager approval reference
🇵🇭 Philippines: Withdrawal friction is the highest churn driver for PH VIP players. GCash and Maya withdrawals should settle within 2–4 hours for Gold+ players. If a bank transfer is delayed, a personal explanation from the host with a cashback gesture goes a long way. Never let a VIP player chase a withdrawal without proactive contact first.

06
Compliance & Regulatory Obligations
EDD for high-value players · AMLC CTR filing · Responsible gambling · PAGCOR requirements
KYC Senior + CCO
▾
Compliance Obligations
  • EDD Trigger: Any player nominated for Gold tier or above must have a completed Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) file before VIP benefits are activated — no exceptions. VAM must not make first contact until KYC Senior confirms EDD is complete
  • AMLC CTR: Single or linked transactions ≥ ₱500,000 in any banking day must be reported to AMLC as a Cash Transaction Report. VIP team must alert Compliance immediately when a player hits this threshold
  • AMLC STR: If the VIP team observes unusual transaction patterns (e.g. large deposits followed by immediate withdrawals without play, third-party deposits, unexplained source of funds), raise a Suspicious Activity flag to the KYC Senior within 24 hours. Do not confront the player
  • Periodic EDD Review: All Gold+ players undergo a full EDD refresh every 12 months, or immediately if deposit volume increases by >100% in any 30-day period
  • PAGCOR VEP Check: Re-run PAGCOR Voluntary Exclusion Programme check at each tier upgrade — not only at initial KYC. A self-excluded player must never receive VIP benefits
Responsible Gambling Obligations
  • VAM must complete the internal RG awareness training before managing any VIP player
  • Large loss sessions: if a player loses ≥ ₱100,000 in a single session, the VAM check-in must include a welfare question. Do not offer a bonus as the first message — lead with genuine concern
  • If a player asks to set a deposit limit or expresses distress: immediately escalate to the Responsible Gambling Specialist. Do not attempt to retain the player before RG has spoken with them
  • Any VIP player who self-excludes must be removed from the VIP programme immediately. No contact, no re-engagement, no bonuses. Forward to RG Specialist for proper offboarding
  • Monitor for PAGCOR GPAP (Gaming Protection and Awareness Programme) compliance requirements — all VIP interactions must comply with PAGCOR RG standards
🇵🇭 PAGCOR: PAGCOR requires that licensed operators have documented RG procedures for high-value players. The VIP team's approach to large loss sessions must be recorded in the CRM. PAGCOR auditors may request evidence of player welfare actions during licence renewal reviews.
Regulators: AMLC — CTR/STR Portal PAGCOR — VEP Check KYC Case Management RG Specialist Handoff

07
VIP Offboarding
Graceful downgrade · Voluntary exit · Compliance-triggered closure · Self-exclusion
VIP Manager + Compliance
▾
Offboarding Scenarios
  • Natural downgrade: Volume drops below Bronze threshold for 2 consecutive months. Player transitions to standard player status. VAM sends a farewell message acknowledging their time as a VIP and explaining how they can rejoin
  • Player request: Player asks to be removed from VIP programme or personal host contact. Honour immediately. Remove from all VIP CRM campaigns. Log reason. Do not re-enrol without player's explicit opt-in
  • Self-exclusion / RG exit: Immediate removal from all VIP benefits and communications. Forward to RG Specialist. No re-engagement permitted under any circumstances while exclusion is active
  • Compliance closure: Account closed by Compliance (AML, fraud, or KYC failure). VIP programme access terminated immediately. VAM must not contact the player once a compliance hold is placed — refer all queries to Compliance team
Offboarding Checklist
  • Remove player from all active VIP bonus campaigns and scheduled touchpoints in the CRM
  • Cancel any outstanding scheduled gifts or event invitations
  • Ensure all pending cashback is calculated and paid out per the player's entitlement at exit date
  • Log the exit reason, date, and responsible manager in the VIP CRM profile
  • Notify the Payments team to process any outstanding withdrawal at standard priority (or priority if RG-related)
  • Archive the VIP preference profile for potential future reactivation (unless compliance or RG closure — in which case, flag profile as permanently closed)
  • Conduct a brief internal debrief if the player was Platinum or Elite — capture learning points for the VIP team

Archived — Reactivation EligibleProfile kept for 12 months. Automated re-engagement trigger if player returns and deposits at qualifying volume

Permanently ClosedRG self-exclusion or compliance closure. Profile flagged. No re-enrolment. Stored per AMLC 5-year retention requirement

Reference Document

VIP Glossary

Key terms, acronyms, metrics, and Philippines-specific context for the VIP management workflow.

VIP Operations Terms
NGR
Net Gaming Revenue
Total player deposits minus withdrawals, minus bonuses paid. The primary metric for assessing a VIP player's value. NGR is used to determine tier eligibility and cashback entitlement.
GGR
Gross Gaming Revenue
Total wagers placed minus total winnings paid out, before deducting bonuses or operating costs. Used for broader financial reporting alongside NGR.
LTV
Lifetime Value
The projected total NGR a player will generate over their active lifetime on the platform. Used to justify retention investment — a high LTV player warrants more aggressive retention spend than their current month's NGR alone suggests.
VAM
VIP Account Manager
The dedicated personal host assigned to a Gold, Platinum, or Elite VIP player. Responsible for all personal touchpoints, offers, gift management, and first-line escalation for the assigned player portfolio.
Cashback
Loss Rebate Bonus
A percentage of a player's net losses returned to them as a bonus, typically on a weekly or monthly basis depending on tier. Cashback rates are negotiated at Platinum and Elite tier. All cashback must be processed through the bonus engine with full documentation.
Churn
Player Lapse / Attrition
A VIP player who has become inactive (no deposits for 30+ days) or who has materially reduced their deposit volume. Churn prevention is the primary measure of VIP team performance — retaining an existing VIP is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring a new one.
EDD
Enhanced Due Diligence
A deeper identity and source of funds verification required for all Gold+ VIP players before benefits are activated. Includes employment verification, bank statements, and source of funds declaration. Mandatory under AMLC regulations for high-value customers.
🇵🇭 AMLC Requirement
CTR
Cash Transaction Report
Filed with AMLC when a player's single or linked transactions reach ₱500,000 or more in a single banking day. The VIP team must alert Compliance the same day when a player hits this threshold — do not wait for end-of-day reporting.
🇵🇭 AMLC Filing
STR
Suspicious Transaction Report
Filed with AMLC when there is reasonable suspicion that a transaction involves proceeds of an unlawful activity. VIP team flags suspicious patterns to the KYC Senior who prepares the STR for CCO submission. Tipping-off the player is a criminal offence.
🇵🇭 AMLC Filing
Pakikisama
Filipino Cultural Value
A Filipino concept meaning camaraderie, solidarity, and getting along well with others. In the context of VIP management, it means building genuine personal relationships rather than purely transactional interactions. PH VIP players respond significantly better to hosts who demonstrate authentic care.
🇵🇭 Cultural Context
GPAP
Gaming Protection and Awareness Programme
PAGCOR's responsible gambling framework that all licensed operators must comply with. Includes requirements for player self-exclusion, deposit limits, loss limits, and mandatory RG awareness messaging. VIP teams are subject to GPAP obligations in all high-value player interactions.
🇵🇭 PAGCOR Requirement
Payday Cycle
PH Deposit Peak Days
In the Philippines, salary payments are typically made on the 15th and last day of each month (semi-monthly pay schedule). These are the highest deposit days in the calendar. VIP teams should plan personalised outreach and offers around these dates for maximum engagement impact.
🇵🇭 Market Context
Philippines Payment Context
GCash
Mynt / Globe Fintech
The dominant e-wallet platform in the Philippines with over 90 million registered users. The primary deposit and withdrawal method for the majority of VIP players. GCash transactions are near-instant. VAMs must understand GCash limits — personal accounts have a ₱100,000 wallet limit; players may need a GCash Business account for higher volumes.
🇵🇭 Primary E-Wallet
Maya
PayMaya / PLDT Voyager
The second major e-wallet platform in the Philippines. Popular among more financially sophisticated players and those with business accounts. Maya supports higher transaction limits than standard GCash. Platinum and Elite players may prefer Maya for large single transactions.
🇵🇭 Secondary E-Wallet
InstaPay / PESONet
BSP Interbank Payment Systems
BSP-regulated interbank fund transfer systems. InstaPay is real-time (24/7, up to ₱50,000 per transaction). PESONet is batch-cleared (business day). Used for bank-to-bank VIP withdrawals. VAMs should understand which system is being used as it affects the settlement timeline communicated to the player.
